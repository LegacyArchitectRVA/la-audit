## What Is a Life Manual?

A Life Manual is the operational layer between your life and the people who would need to manage it in your absence. It answers one question: **If you were suddenly unavailable, could someone step in and keep everything running?**

Unlike estate planning, which determines *who receives assets*, a Life Manual explains *how things actually work*. Unlike password managers, which store credentials, a Life Manual provides *context* about what matters and what to do.

**Full deliverable:** Formatted Life Manual (PDF) + Secure Digital Vault + Configured Password Manager

## The 7-Pillar Framework

Every Life Manual is organized around 7 pillars of continuity:

| Pillar | What It Covers |
|--------|---------------|
| **1. Digital Life** | Email access, password vault, cloud storage, 2FA recovery, social media legacy |
| **2. Emergency & Successor Access** | Who steps in, authority documentation, first 72-hour instructions |
| **3. Financial & Assets** | Bank accounts, investments, insurance, debts, tax records |
| **4. Household & Property** | Utilities, maintenance, vendors, property records, vehicles |
| **5. Health & Medical** | Providers, medications, directives, insurance, emergency protocols |
| **6. Business Continuity** | Operations, succession, key relationships, vendor contracts *(Legacy tier)* |
| **7. Legacy & Wishes** | Personal letters, memorial preferences, charitable intentions |
